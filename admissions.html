Admission Requirements.

  • Completed admission form (Download or collect from office).
  • Copy of Birth Certificate
  • Latest school report or exam results.
  • 2 Passport-size photos.
  • Medical form (available at school).

How to Apply.

  1. Download the admission form.
  2. Fill out the form and attach required documents.
  3. Submit in person or email to: admissions@stjune.ac.ke.
  4. Wait for official confirmation and schedule interview if required.

Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)

1. When do admissions open?

Admissions are open throughout the year, subject to available space.

2. Do you offer scholarships?

Yes. We offer merit-based scholarships for outstanding students.

3. Is boarding available?

Yes. Boarding is available for upper primary and secondary students.
