Our Academic Program.

At St. June Academy, we provide a strong academic foundation tailored to every stage of your child's development. Our programs focus on nurturing creativity, curiosity, and confidence. Through interactive learning and a caring environment, students grow intellectually, socially, and emotionally. We ensure each learner thrives from early years to graduation.

Kindergarten.

Playful learning for a strong start.

Our Kindergarten program combines fun with early education. Children are introduced to letters, numbers, and creative play in a safe, loving environment. Social interaction and hands-on learning spark curiosity and build confidence from the very beginning.

Junior School.

Expanding minds through active learning.

In Junior School, students build on core subjects through interactive lessons that promote problem-solving, teamwork, and self-expression. Our holistic approach helps learners grow academically while exploring their interests and talents.

Senior School.

Leadership, excellence, and preparation.

Senior School focuses on developing critical thinking, discipline, and leadership. We prepare students for national exams and beyond, empowering them with the tools to succeed in higher education and life.
